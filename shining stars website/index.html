ABOUT US

Meet the proprietress

The proprietress, Mrs Akinbobola Yemisi, is a God-fearing and enthusiastic educator. She has always had a special love educating young people which led her to starting a tutorial center for students who are planning to sit for WAEC, JAMB, NECO ,GCE and some other forms of exams. Then in the month of July during the year 2009, she decided to take this the love further by raising the structures and foundations of the school you know today to be Shining stars college. With the grace and mercies of God for more than a decade, she and her loyal teachers have been impacting the lives of the students of Shining stars college (both graduated and current) academically and spiritually.

Mrs Akinbobola Yemisi has a HND in computer science (From the polytechnic of Ibadan), a Postgraduate Diploma in Education and Masters in Business Administration (From the University of Ibadan) and finally, a Postgradute Diploma in Business Administration (From the University of Calabar). She is a disciplinarian and a person who believes that her students should see good morals and values coupled with good academic grades as a priority.

ADMISSIONS

Dear Prospective Parents,

Thank you for your interest in Shining Stars Schools!

It is of great joy to us that you have chosen to partner with us in giving your child(ren) the gift of education out of the countless options.

We know how stressful it is for you as parent to find the ideal school for your little angels or your curious adolescents. For this very reason we have taken it upon ourselves to make sure that you choosing to partner with us wouldn't be a mistake.


The phone numbers of our admins will always be available,and the admins will always be ready to speak to you and help you through the admission phase. If by chance you would be busy for calls , our email and whatsapp chat space would readily be available for you.

Application for admission will only be processed when all the required and neccessary documents as listed by the admins and the neccessarytests have been carried out. Once your child(ren) has been offered admission , acceptances indicated by the payment of required fees.
N.B: NO STUDENT WILL BE CONSIDERED ENROLLED UNTIL ALL REQUIRED DOCUMENTS HAS BEEN RETURNED TO THE SCHOOL.
